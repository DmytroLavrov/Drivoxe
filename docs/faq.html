    Frequently Asked Questions

    Booking and Reservations

    How do I book a car with Drivoxe?

    Booking with Drivoxe is easy. Visit our website or app, select your preferred car, set your dates, and complete the reservation in a few clicks.

    Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

    Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation. Log in to your account and follow the prompts for reservation changes.

    What are the age requirements for renting a car?

    To rent with Drivoxe, you must be at least 21 years old. Certain premium and specialty cars may have higher age requirements.

    Are there any hidden fees in the rental cost?

    No hidden fees. Drivoxe provides transparent pricing. Your rental cost covers the vehicle, taxes, and standard insurance. Additional options may be available.

    How can I extend my rental period?

    You can request an extension by contacting our customer support team. We’ll assist you in extending your rental based on availability.

    Car Pickup and Return

    Where can I pick up my rental car?

    You can pick up your rental car from one of our conveniently located branches or have it delivered to your specified location.

    What do I need to bring for car pickup?

    Bring your driver’s license, a valid payment method, and any required identification, such as a passport for international travelers.

    What’s the process for returning the car?

    Return the car to the designated location at the agreed time. Our team will inspect the vehicle, and you’ll receive a final invoice.

    Can I return the car after business hours?

    In some locations, we offer after-hours return options. Contact our team for details and instructions.

    Do you offer one-way rentals?

    Yes, one-way rentals are available with Drivoxe. Additional fees may apply, depending on the drop-off location, ensuring flexibility and convenience for your travel needs.

    Payment and Billing

    What payment methods are accepted?

    We accept major credit and debit cards, as well as cash payments in some locations. Check our website for specific details.

    Is a security deposit required?

    Yes, a security deposit is typically required to cover incidentals. The amount varies depending on the car and rental duration, ensuring peace of mind and coverage during your rental period.

    How is the rental cost calculated?

    Rental costs are calculated based on the car you choose, the rental duration, and any additional services or coverage you select.

    Can I use my own insurance for the rental?

    Yes, you can use your personal insurance, but you may still need to provide proof and accept responsibility for any damages during the rental.

    What if I have a question about my billing statement?

    If you have questions or concerns about your billing, please contact our customer support team, and we’ll be happy to assist you.

    Additional Services

    Do you offer child safety seats?

    Yes, we provide child safety seats upon request. Please let us know your requirements when making your reservation.

    Can I add an additional driver to the rental?

    Yes, additional drivers can be added to the rental contract. Be sure to provide their information when you pick up the car.

    Do you offer GPS navigation systems?

    Yes, we offer GPS navigation systems for a daily fee. Request one when booking your car or upon pickup.

    What should I do in case of an accident or breakdown?

    In case of an accident or breakdown, contact our emergency assistance line immediately. We’ll guide you through the necessary steps.

    Are pets allowed in the rental cars?

    While we love pets, we kindly request that you refrain from bringing them into the rental cars, with the exception of service animals.
